About the club

Cloud, learned by doing

We're a student-run chapter that exists for one reason: get more SKIT students building on AWS, faster. No lecture-only sessions — every meetup ends with something deployed, broken, or shipped.

From your first EC2 instance to your first Solutions Architect certification, we run the workshops, mentor the projects, and host the hackathons that get you there — backed by the AWS Educate and AWS Academy programs.

Whether you're a first-year who's never opened a terminal or a senior chasing a cloud role, there's a track for you here.
